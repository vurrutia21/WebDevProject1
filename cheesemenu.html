Cheese Menu

Charcuterie board comes with choice of 2 cheeses for $10 or all 4 cheeses for $17. Served with mixed fruit, jelly, crackers or bread

Brie

A soft cheese that pairs beautifully with a Pinot Noir or Chardonnay. P.S.- you can eat the rind!

Gouda

A semi-hard cheese that is a perfect pairing with most wines: Cabernet Sauvignon, Pinot Noir, Pinot Grigio, Chardonnay, Riesling.

Cheddar

A hard, aged cheese that best pairs with Cabernet Sauvignon.

Manchego

A hard cheese that pairs nicely with drier reds and whites.

Gruyere

The hard cheese that is both a red and white's best friend!
